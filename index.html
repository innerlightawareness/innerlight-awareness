About Me

My work is rooted in understanding patterns, timing, and lived experience, using astrology and intuitive insight to bring clarity to the deeper themes shaping life.

At some point, most of us begin to notice that life does not unfold randomly. Certain experiences repeat, and some phases feel difficult to understand even when we are trying to move forward.

This question has been central to my own life as well. Why do certain experiences repeat, even when we are trying to move forward, and what are they asking us to understand?

My search for these answers led me deeper into astrology, not just as a subject, but as a way to understand the underlying structure of life.

Alongside this, I became aware that I naturally perceive things beyond what is immediately visible. Over time, this intuitive and almost psychic sensitivity became an integral part of how I understand patterns, situations, and people.

At the same time, I come from a strong academic background in mathematics, statistics, and computer science, and I have worked in corporate and government environments. This keeps my approach grounded. I do not rely on blind belief. I need to understand what I am seeing.

But what has shaped my work the most is my own life.

I have gone through phases that brought emotional intensity, instability, health challenges, and situations that did not make sense even when I was doing everything right. Those phases forced me to question everything I thought I knew about effort, control, and outcome.

That is where my understanding deepened.

I began to see that what we go through is not random. There are patterns. There is timing. There are karmic imprints that continue to play out until something is seen and understood.

For me, astrology is not about predicting events or offering quick remedies.

It is about understanding the theme behind the situation.

When you understand the theme and the underlying karmic pattern, things begin to make sense. You start to see what is repeating, what is actually driving the situation, and what needs to shift.

My work is not about fixing your life from the outside.

It is about helping you see what your life is trying to show you.

Because once you see it clearly, your response changes. Your choices change. And that is what begins to shift the pattern.

And often, when something is seen with that level of clarity, healing does not have to be forced. It begins on its own.

I do use astrology, numerology, and other systems, but I do not follow a fixed method. I use what is needed to understand the situation fully, along with my intuitive reading.

What I offer is not surface level guidance.

I help you understand:

  • why certain patterns or karmic patterns are repeating
  • what phase you are currently in
  • what is actually driving the situation
  • what may be required from you at this point
  • what needs to be done differently
  • how to respond with clarity instead of confusion

At a deeper level, my work is about awareness.

Because real change does not come from being told what to do.

It comes when you see something so clearly that you cannot go back to responding in the same way.

I work with a limited number of people so that each session is focused, prepared, and specific.
